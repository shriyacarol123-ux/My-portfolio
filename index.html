📊 Personal Health Vault

Think of this as the app's persistent data vault. It securely maps your daily caloric intake to your unique username in the MySQL database, ensuring your energy balance is always up-to-date and ready for analysis.

🍎 Smart Meal Tracker

This part of the app acts like a digital assistant. Every time you type in a meal, it instantly captures the information, calculates your progress, and ensures your data is sent to the right place.
